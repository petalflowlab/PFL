The Next Step

Meet V2.

Two years of hard use led to two years of improvements. Click each feature below to learn what changed — and why it matters.

Industry standard tough. The 3-piece design with long overlaps avoids flexing problems, ensuring the electronics inside stay protected no matter what.

More LEDs meant more power drain, so a full-size 18650 battery was chosen to add weight and provide enough power. Our target is at least 10 hours of flow on a charge — USB-C charging won't be needed til morning.

The petals have endured over two years of use, though the largest ones were always a struggle to survive the hard life of smashing on the ground. Silicone molded petals improve strength significantly so you won't need to worry anymore.

A custom PCB with a 3-axis accelerometer and gyroscope tracks your exact speed, tilt, and rotation in real time. The LEDs respond to what you're actually doing — not a preset loop. Fast spin triggers a bloom. A slow roll closes it back down. It knows the difference.

A built-in microphone syncs the LEDs to the music around you in real time. Beat drops trigger flashes, pulses, and reactive waves across the full LED strip. Dial the sensitivity through the app and let the room control your lights.

What Is This Kickstarter Campaign Structure?

These are completely custom-built props — from the petal head design to the electronics to final assembly. Every part of it came from scratch. During prototyping, they've been taken apart and put back together again and again, which makes a kickass demo prop but unsuitable for your hands.

From prototype to final durable reliable staff needs two dramatic changes. Redesigned PCB layout to strengthen LED attachment point and stronger silicone molded Big Flower Staff Petals. . All components from previous capsule is remaining the same, only the aforenentioned structural changes are being made so the year of testing still holds up.

  • 1. Direct PCB Connection — Strengthened connection of LED strip to board for zero wiggle (fixing V1 wiggles).
  • 2. Silicone Molded Petals — Superior strength to guaranteed they survive the hard life of drops (fixing prototype casualties).
  • 3. 3-Piece Design — Remaining stiff using polycarbonate overlap while adding portability and independent battery/sensor tracking.
